How It Works

Owner

Owner will list their parking space for desired rate, at a desired schedule.

Driver

Drivers will find parking spaces, will make escrow payment and Checks in, checks out. He will gave feedback and release the Escrow. Or he can make Dispute, parking.com site will handle it.

Payment

The escrow will release to owner user profile in parking.com. The amount in the parking.com account will transfer to the owner's paypal account on monthly basis.



Share Your Space

About

You will create an account and list your parking space address. Then you will set your schedule, along with dynamic market rate pricing based on time of day, location, etc. You can modify or suspend your space if your schedule changes. Your parking space will now show up on the market only during those times. People search for available spaces on Park Circa, if your parking space is reasonably priced and the maximum remaining time fits their needs, they will book the parking space and park in the space. You will get paid montly based on the parking.
